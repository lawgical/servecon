About ServeCon

ServeCon is an online conference offered to all members of the process serving profession. If you are looking for the latest information on running a business, marketing, technology, software, legislation, and the importance of association membership, this is the conference for you. Check back for more information, including updates on speakers and interactive workshops.

Why Attend ServeCon?

Presented using the latest remote conference technology, ServeCon gives attendees with access to engaging speakers and interactive workshops all from the comfort of their home or office. Don't worry about the cost of airfare, food, and lodging, because we are bringing the hottest industry speakers to you, wherever you are. This is a great way to get educated without the added expense and stress of travel.
